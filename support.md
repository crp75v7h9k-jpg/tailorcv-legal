## Common Questions

### How does TailorCV work?
1. Upload your resume (PDF or DOCX) or paste it as text.
2. Paste a job description from any job board (LinkedIn, Indeed, company site, etc.).
3. Tap **Tailor** — TailorCV's AI rewrites your bullets to match the role, scores you against ATS filters, and highlights missing keywords.
4. Optionally generate a tailored cover letter.
5. Export the result as a polished PDF.

### Is TailorCV free?
You can tailor a limited number of resumes for free. **TailorCV Pro** unlocks unlimited tailoring, PDF export, AI cover letters, and detailed ATS breakdowns.

### How much does Pro cost?
- **Yearly** — ₺1.499,99 (best value)
- **Weekly** — ₺199,99
- Includes a **3-day free trial** for new subscribers

Subscription auto-renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel any time in iOS **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**.

### My PDF upload isn't working
Make sure your PDF is text-based and not a scanned image. If your resume was scanned, run it through any OCR tool first, or paste the text into the App directly.

### My ATS score seems low
TailorCV scores how well your resume matches the specific job description you provided. A low score on one job doesn't mean your resume is bad — it means it doesn't match THIS specific role's keywords yet. Try tapping **Tailor** to let the AI optimize it.

### Can I edit the AI's suggestions?
Yes — every bullet rewrite shows you the original and the AI version side by side. You can accept, reject, or edit any suggestion before exporting.

### How do I export my tailored resume?
Open any saved result → tap **Export PDF**. The file saves to your Files app or can be shared via any messaging or email app.

## Subscriptions

### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open iOS **Settings**.
2. Tap your name at the top → **Subscriptions**.
3. Select **TailorCV** → **Cancel Subscription**.
4. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your current billing period.

### How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request a refund at:
https://reportaproblem.apple.com

### My free trial ended and I was charged
Free trials auto-convert to paid subscriptions unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the trial ends. To cancel a trial, follow the cancel steps above before the trial expires.

### Restore Purchases
If you previously subscribed and the App doesn't recognize it (e.g. after reinstalling), tap **Restore** at the bottom of the paywall screen.

## Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

**To delete your account:**
1. Email **support@tailorcv.app** from the email address associated with your account, with the subject **"Delete My Account"**.
2. We will delete all your data (account, saved resumes, subscription records) within 30 days and confirm via email when complete.

In-app account deletion is coming in a future update.
